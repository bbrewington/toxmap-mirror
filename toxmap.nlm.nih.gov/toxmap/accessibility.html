TOXMAP Accessibility

Section 508 of the Rehabilitation Act requires that members of the public with disabilities and Federal Government employees with disabilities have access to and use of information and data comparable to individuals who do not have disabilities.


If a disability is preventing you from using TOXMAP in its current form, please contact us and we will provide assistance directly. Alternatively, consider using the text-only resources listed below that provide much of the same information.


For more information, refer to the National Library of Medicine's Accessibility Information page.


TOXMAP's data are also available in text-only format from a variety of sources:

  • TOXNET®/TRI (National Library of Medicine);
  • HSDB® (National Library of Medicine);
  • TRI Explorer (U.S. EPA);
  • USA Quick Facts (U.S. Census Bureau); and
  • The Agency for Toxic Substances and Disease Registry.
